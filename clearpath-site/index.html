The Compass Pathway

Behavior & Compliance Solutions

Our flagship pathway provides campuses and districts with proven tools to manage, document, and improve student behavior outcomes — while maintaining full compliance with state and federal requirements. Three powerful products. One clear direction.

📍

Waypoint

Texas DAEP compliance management — from incident documentation and approval chains to SPED compliance blocking, parent portals, and transition plans. See it in action below.

Starting at $4,500/yr
🧭

Navigator

ISS and OSS management built for campus administrators — track referrals, document placements, assign proactive supports, and catch escalation patterns before they reach DAEP.

Starting at $2,500/yr
  • Referral intake and admin review workflow
  • ISS / OSS placement tracking with re-entry plans
  • Proactive supports: CICO, behavior contracts, counseling
  • Escalation alerts (3+ OSS in 90 days → DAEP review)
  • Student behavior risk scoring
  • Referral trend reports & offense code analytics
⭐

Meridian

A comprehensive SPED compliance engine to help campuses reach peak performance — ensuring every policy, plan, and practice aligns with state and federal requirements.

Starting at $3,500/yr
  • PBIS & MTSS framework alignment
  • FBA/BIP development & review support
  • Disciplinary compliance auditing
  • Campus systems coaching
