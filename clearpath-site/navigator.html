What Navigator Does
Everything you need to manage campus-level discipline — in one place.

Teacher Referral Intake

Urgency levels, concern categories, and campus routing — teachers document, administrators triage.

ISS/OSS Placement Tracking

Active, completed, and overdue placements with days served. Know who's where and for how long.

Student Risk Scoring

0–100 risk score based on referral frequency, suspensions, and attendance patterns. Prioritize who needs help now.

Escalation Engine

Flags ISS → OSS → DAEP patterns before they happen. Early warning gives you time to intervene.

Support Assignments

Track interventions, assigned staff, and effectiveness measurement. Know what's working and what isn't.

Skill Gap Map

Identifies which behavioral skills need campus-wide intervention based on referral patterns.

Disproportionality Radar

Surfaces equity concerns by race, gender, and SPED status. Catch disproportionate discipline before TEA does.

Campus Goals

Set targets, track progress, and compare year-over-year. Data-driven discipline improvement for your campus.
