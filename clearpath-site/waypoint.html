What Waypoint Does
Every step of the discipline lifecycle, covered.

Incident Management

Create, route, and track discipline incidents with role-based workflows. Teachers document, administrators decide.

SPED/504 Compliance Blocking

Automatically blocks DAEP placement until manifestation determination is complete. Prevents TEA violations before they happen.

Multi-Role Approval Chain

Sequential 6-role approval: CBC → Counselor → SPED → 504 → SSS → Director. Each step documented with timestamps.

10-Day Removal Tracking

Cumulative removal days tracked per SPED student with auto-alerts at day 7, 9, and 10. IDEA §300.536 compliance built in.

Parent Portal

Parents view incidents, acknowledge notices, and track DAEP placement status. Documented proof of notification for due process.

Transition Plans & Reviews

Structured re-entry plans with 30/60/90-day review cycles. 4-party readiness checklist ensures nothing is missed.

PEIMS Export

One-click export in TEA PEIMS format. SIS integration presets for Skyward, PowerSchool, Infinite Campus, and TxEIS.

Daily Behavior Kiosk

DAEP campus staff track daily behavior, orientation completion, and attendance via a shared kiosk — no individual logins needed.

Compliance Dashboard

District-wide view of every campus: overdue MDRs, approaching 10-day limits, pending checklists — all in one place.
