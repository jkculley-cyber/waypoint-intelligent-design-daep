# Waypoint — AI Presenter Demo Video
## Full Production Package

**Format:** AI presenter + screen recording B-roll (HeyGen / Synthesia / ElevenLabs)
**Target length:** 2 minutes 25 seconds
**Audience:** District administrators + DAEP campus staff
**Tone:** Warm, confident, human — compliance authority without cold corporate energy
**CTA:** Visit clearpathedgroup.com to request a free demo

---

## Production Notes (Read Before Recording)

### Presenter Selection
- Choose a warm, authoritative voice — professional but not stiff
- Test both female and male options; female has tested warmer in EdTech
- Pace: **135 words per minute** — slightly slower than default (gives weight to compliance points)
- Emotion dial: 70% warm/reassuring, 30% confident/authoritative
- NO robotic cadence — if the AI voice sounds stiff on a sentence, break it into two shorter ones

### Visual Layout
- Left 2/3 of frame: screen recording B-roll
- Right 1/3 of frame: AI presenter talking head (or full-screen presenter for emotional scenes)
- Switch to full-screen presenter for the Hook (Scene 1), the SPED block moment (Scene 5), and the Close (Scene 9)
- Color palette: deep purple `#6D28D9`, white, warm gray — matches the Waypoint app

### Screen Recordings Needed (B-roll list)
1. Waypoint dashboard — active placements, alert badges visible
2. New incident form — filling out offense code, campus, student name
3. Compliance checklist auto-appearing after incident creation
4. SPED hard-block modal — red warning, "Cannot proceed until manifestation determination is complete"
5. DAEP Dashboard — orientation status, 30-day review countdown, behavior scoring
6. Parent portal — student's placement card, behavior score chart
7. Reports page — PEIMS export button click → file downloads
