## 1. Purpose

This Agreement governs the processing of student education records by Navigator, a student behavior tracking platform provided by Clear Path Education Group, LLC. Navigator processes student discipline data (referrals, ISS/OSS placements, behavioral supports) on behalf of the School/District for the purpose of improving student outcomes and ensuring compliance with discipline policies.

## 2. Data Collected

Navigator processes the following student data:

- Student name, grade level, campus
- Discipline referrals (date, location, description, outcome)
- ISS and OSS placement records
- Behavioral support assignments and effectiveness data
- Special population flags (SPED, Section 504, ELL status)
- Staff names associated with referrals and supports

Navigator does **not** collect: Social Security numbers, medical records, financial information, biometric data, or parent/guardian PII beyond what is included in placement notifications.

## 3. FERPA Compliance

Clear Path Education Group acts as a "school official" with a "legitimate educational interest" under FERPA (34 CFR 99.31(a)(1)). The Processor agrees to:

- Use student data **only** for the purposes described in this Agreement
- **Not** disclose student data to any third party without prior written consent
- **Not** use student data for marketing, advertising, or profiling
- Maintain reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards
- Delete all student data within 30 days of contract termination upon written request

## 4. Texas Student Data Privacy (TEC 32.151-32.157)

The Processor complies with the Texas Education Code provisions on student data privacy:

- Student data is stored in the United States on Supabase cloud infrastructure (AWS us-east-1)
- The Processor will not sell or rent student data
- The Processor will provide written notice of any data breach within 48 hours
- The School/District retains ownership of all student data at all times

## 5. Security Measures

- All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256)
- Row-Level Security enforces tenant isolation at the database level
- Access limited to authenticated, authorized school/district staff
- No student data is accessible to other schools or districts

## 6. Term and Termination

This Agreement is effective for the duration of the Navigator subscription. Upon termination, the Processor will delete all student data within 30 days of written request or provide a data export in standard format (CSV/Excel).
