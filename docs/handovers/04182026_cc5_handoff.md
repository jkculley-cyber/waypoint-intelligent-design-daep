## Lessons This Session

- **Put compliance exports where counselors already go for the underlying data.** SB 179 belonged on Time Tracker, not a document-templates page. The template page is for documents the app *doesn't* auto-derive (permission slips, parent letters, safety screenings).
- **Preset chips + a single "Custom" escape hatch beat a full date picker.** 90% of compliance reports hit one of three cadences (month / semester / year). Always showing a from/to picker makes the simple case slower.
