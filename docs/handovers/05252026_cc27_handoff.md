## What was done

### 1. Schema-drift CI extended with REQUIRED_ROWS

`scripts/check-schema-drift.mjs` — CC26 spent multiple rounds debugging because the audit sentinel UUID (`profiles.id = '00000000-...'`) had drifted out of production despite migration 071 supposedly seeding it, and column-only drift checks couldn't catch a missing row. New `REQUIRED_ROWS` section probes load-bearing seed rows as tripwires. Now passes **14/14** against prod (13 column-shapes + 1 row-invariant).

### 2. Marketing hero carousel deduped

`clearpath-site/index.html` — hero carousel had 4 slides × 2 CTAs each that mirrored the per-product CTAs on the product cards below the fold. Deduped to one high-intent primary CTA per slide ("Get Waypoint Demo →", "Get Navigator Demo →", "Learn About Apex →", "Start Beacon Free Trial →"). Cleaner hero, less competing chrome, one obvious next step per product.

### 3. Waypoint #demo lead-capture form

`clearpath-site/waypoint.html` — the `#demo` section used to show sandbox credentials inline without capturing the visitor. Replaced with a 6-field lead-capture form (Name / Work email / Phone / District / Role / District size) modeled after `navigator.html#pilot`. On submit, three parallel POSTs (Formspree notification + ops `demo_leads` insert + `send-notification` `welcome_demo_request` email) then the Demo Login card + Open Waypoint button reveal inline. Hero subtext updated to match.

### 4. Email template personalization

`supabase/functions/send-notification/index.ts` — `welcome_demo_request` template personalized with `{name}`, matching `welcome_navigator_demo`. Edge Function redeploy treated as optional polish; the existing template still works without it (just no "Hi {Name}" greeting).
