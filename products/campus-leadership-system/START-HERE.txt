━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
EXCEL TRACKERS (open in Excel or Google Sheets)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. Admin-Command-Toolkit-Texas-Edition.xlsx  ← START HERE
   Your command center. Enter incidents here, everything else
   auto-populates. 14 tabs including Command Dashboard,
   Incident Intake, Investigation Tracker, 10-Day Compliance,
   Evidence Tracker, Parent Contact Log (15 phone scripts),
   Discipline Trends, and Monday Briefing.

2. DAEP-Student-Tracker-Texas-Edition.xlsx
   Track DAEP placements, compliance deadlines, and MDR flags —
   mandatory placements under TEC §37.006 and discretionary
   placements under your district's Student Code of Conduct.

3. ISS-Intervention-Tracker-Texas-Edition.xlsx
   Track ISS placements with daily intervention logs, 8
   intervention tracks, student activity plans by offense,
   re-entry checklists, and outcome measurement.

4. TEC-Consequence-Decision-Matrix-Texas-Edition.xlsx
   Reference tool. Every TEC §37 offense with consequence
   options, restorative alternatives, parent scripts, CBC
   workflow, and SPED safeguards.

5. Navigator-Student-Behavior-Tracker-Texas-Edition.xlsx
   RTI/MTSS behavior tracking. Referral log, ISS/OSS
   placements, student supports, risk score calculator.

6. Meridian-SPED-Compliance-Tracker-Texas-Edition.xlsx
   SPED compliance. IEP tracker, 504 tracker, Dyslexia/HB 3928,
   CAP findings, SPPI-13 transition.

RECOMMENDED SETUP ORDER
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Week 1:
  Open Admin-Command-Toolkit.xlsx
  Fill in the Cover tab (district name, campus, school year)
  Start logging incidents in the Incident Intake tab

Week 2:
  Open each specialist tracker (DAEP, ISS, Navigator, Meridian)
  Fill in their Cover tabs
  Link student IDs consistently across all files

Ongoing:
  Use the Digital Form for every new incident
  Export to Toolkit with one click
  Check the Command Dashboard Monday mornings
